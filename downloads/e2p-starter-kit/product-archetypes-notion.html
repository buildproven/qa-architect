Product Archetype Cheat Sheet

7 Patterns That Work for Domain Experts

Your expertise isn't too niche. It's a moat. These 7 patterns show you how domain experts have turned career knowledge into repeatable revenue — and how you can too.

ARCHETYPE 1
Compliance & Risk Checker
Scans documents, processes, or systems against a known standard and flags what's wrong.
Real Example An ASPICE compliance checker that reviews automotive software documentation and highlights gaps against the standard — work that takes a consultant days, done in minutes.
"You know the standard cold. AI can check against it. Nobody without your expertise could build the right checklist."
Revenue Model SaaS subscription
Typical Price $199–$999/mo
Build Time 4–8 weeks
Best if: Your expertise involves standards, regulations, certifications, audits, or best practices.
ARCHETYPE 2
Workflow Automator
Takes a multi-step expert process and automates it end-to-end — "give me X, I'll handle the rest."
Real Example A procurement risk assessment tool that takes a supplier list, runs due diligence checks, and outputs a scored report — replacing 3 days of analyst work.
"You've done this process hundreds of times. You know every edge case. AI executes the steps; your expertise defines what those steps are."
Revenue Model Per-use or sub
Typical Price $49–$299/mo
Build Time 6–10 weeks
Best if: Your work involves a repeatable process that produces a deliverable — report, plan, assessment, recommendation.
ARCHETYPE 3
Knowledge Codifier
Captures expert knowledge — your frameworks, heuristics, decision trees — and makes it accessible as a tool or assistant.
Real Example A medical specialist's diagnostic framework, turned into a clinical decision support tool that guides less experienced practitioners through the same reasoning process.
"The knowledge is yours. Nobody else can build this with the same depth or accuracy."
Revenue Model SaaS or licensing
Typical Price $99–$499/mo
Build Time 4–12 weeks
Best if: People regularly ask you the same questions, or pay for your opinion on complex decisions.
ARCHETYPE 4
Decision Engine
Takes a set of inputs and recommends what to do — replacing the "should we do X?" conversation with a data-driven answer.
Real Example A supply chain disruption tool that analyzes your supply chain profile and recommends mitigation strategies — prioritized by risk and cost — rather than leaving the decision to gut feel.
"The decision logic is your expertise. AI applies it consistently and at scale."
Revenue Model SaaS subscription
Typical Price $199–$1,499/mo
Build Time 6–10 weeks
Best if: Your clients frequently face the same type of decision and pay you to help them make it.
ARCHETYPE 5
Data Interpreter
Takes complex, domain-specific data and translates it into plain language, action items, or executive summaries.
Real Example A construction project monitoring tool that takes weekly progress reports and cost data — and produces a plain-English executive summary with risks flagged automatically.
"You know what the data means and what to look for. That interpretation logic is hard to buy anywhere else."
Revenue Model Per-report or sub
Typical Price $99–$499/mo
Build Time 4–8 weeks
Best if: You regularly translate complex data or technical outputs into something non-experts can act on.
ARCHETYPE 6
Training Simulator
Creates realistic practice scenarios that let people develop skills and judgment — without needing access to a senior expert in real time.
Real Example A negotiation training tool for procurement professionals — simulates supplier negotiation scenarios with an AI counterpart that responds like an experienced procurement manager would.
"You know what realistic scenarios look like. You know what good and bad responses sound like. You've made all these mistakes already."
Revenue Model Per-seat SaaS
Typical Price $29–$99/seat/mo
Build Time 8–16 weeks
Best if: Your expertise takes years to develop through experience, and there's a large population trying to learn it.
ARCHETYPE 7
Audit Tool
Systematically reviews existing work — a project, document, system, or process — and identifies what was missed or done wrong.
Real Example A technical specification review tool for medical devices that checks against FDA guidance documents and flags gaps a reviewer might miss under time pressure.
"You've reviewed hundreds of these. You know exactly what goes wrong. That pattern recognition is your product."
Revenue Model Per-audit or sub
Typical Price $199–$999/mo
Build Time 4–8 weeks
Best if: You've been paid (or could be paid) to review other people's work and tell them what they got wrong.

How to Pick Your Pattern

→ My expertise is primarily a set of rules/standards to check against
Start with Archetype 1 (Compliance Checker) or 7 (Audit Tool)
→ My expertise is primarily a repeatable process
Start with Archetype 2 (Workflow Automator)
→ My expertise is primarily knowledge/judgment
Start with Archetype 3 (Knowledge Codifier) or 4 (Decision Engine)
→ My expertise is primarily interpreting complex data
Start with Archetype 5 (Data Interpreter)
→ My expertise took years to develop through lived experience
Consider Archetype 6 (Training Simulator) — it's a bigger build, but high defensibility

The Honest Truth

You don't need to pick the perfect archetype. You need to pick one, build something small, and see if people use it.

The domain experts who win aren't the ones with the best idea. They're the ones who shipped while everyone else was still deciding.
