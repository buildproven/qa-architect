Expertise Audit Worksheet

Turn Your Career Knowledge Into a Product

Most domain experts don't recognise what they know as valuable. They call it "just common sense." It isn't. It's years of hard-won pattern recognition — and someone out there will pay for it.

This worksheet is a 30-minute audit of your career expertise. Do it with a coffee. Be honest with yourself. The goal isn't perfection — it's identifying at least one problem worth building around.
